A Simple Counting Tool for 8mm Cut Tape and Partial Reels

BeanCounter is a simple, inexpensive alternative to traditional SMT component reel counters for hobbyists and small shops with a lot of 8mm wide cut tape and partial reels.

Scroll to learn more.

Pocket-sized, Battery-powered

At only 25x117mm and under a centimeter thick, it's easy to slip in your pocket, carry on a lanyard, or hang from an inventory shelf. It's easily powered by a 2032 coin cell battery.
